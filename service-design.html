Design

Ever been in the situation where the website that looks beautiful on your PC, suddenly looks awful or simply doesn’t work on your phone or tablet? Modern life is all about being on the move. With a big percentage of your website visitors using tablets and mobile phones alongside the more traditional laptop and PC, your digital communications need to work and engage your audience across a range of browsers, platforms and devices.


Responsive Website Designs are backed by a technology that allows a website to maintain the optimal viewing experience on any type of device. Whether you browse using a laptop, tablet or mobile phone the design dynamically alters its resolution to minimize scrolling, panning and resizing of any images. Tablets and mobile phones have surpassed laptop users making responsive website designs a must for any business looking forward.


Well executed design is crucial to effective solutions. Be it a checkout process or the colour of a button, these are all key decisions made during the design process.


We think that whether a logo or layout, good design is where functionality meets aesthetics.
